TERM 2022-2023

The head-honcho of YEF! With his resilience, indomitable talent, and incessant hard work he will inevitably serve as a nonpareil President of Young Economists’ Forum.

The academic maestro, armed with her exuberant persona and unique amalgamation of rigor and amiable nature, our General Secretary is bound to astound us with her top-notch work under all kinds of pressure.

Mashrur Ahmed Zidane

Vice-President (External Operations)

An individual who endured all the adversities and critics to pave the path for reaching the pinnacle, the Vice President of External Operations will surely leave a trace in all the facets of this club.

Musarrat Mahtab

Vice-President (Internal Operations)

With her silver tongued presence and generous heart, alongside her bold gestures and approachable nature, the Vice President of Internal Operations will surely add elan to her and everyone else’s work around her ahead, as always.

Joint Secretary

With her excellent craftsmanship, as well as originality and resourcefulness that leave us in awe, we believe our Joint Secretary will be able to redefine the desired solutions of this club by putting her creative mind to work.

Ertisam Ali Sarker Ifti

The man behind the scene, working fervently and without fail to ensure the timeliness and delivery of perfection, our Chief of Administrative Affair is perhaps who holds up the organization with his unwavering dedication and unmatched zeal.

Rafael Ahmed

Treasurer

A visionary leader and an ingenious marketeer- this individual packs quite a punch with his benevolent presence. We can see the zeal of our Treasurer in all the fields of this club, notably, when it comes to grooming the members through development programs.

We all hold abounding ambitions, but the course of paving the path to reach them is rugged with uphill battles and tough grinds. Most of the time we come across puzzling and unforeseeable trails, and that is exactly when we are obliged to open doors for ourselves and strive to achieve them. Such has been the journey of our Executive Body Members of YEF. It’s not just a name tag, it is the emblem that one is bestowed with to represent YEF and to modulate all the pivotal activities by holding one shared vision, which is to up skill and hone the potential of the members of this club by working collaboratively with them and conveying an apparent idea on the multi-faceted attributes of economics in our lives. These individuals, with their determination and unabated efforts, have finally attained this glorious and illustrious position of Executive Body Member of the Young Economists’ Forum.
