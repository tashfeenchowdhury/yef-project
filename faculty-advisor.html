About our faculty

Honourable Faculty Advisor of the Young Economists’ Forum, Dr Tapas Kumar Paul is the visionary behind the monstrous achievements of the club. Dr Tapas is currently providing his impetus as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Economics, North South University. His academic prudence has helped in maintaining YEF's legacy as the best academic club in North South University. Dr Tapas Kumar Paul comes from a sprawling educational background. He completed his MSc in Economics from the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign and his Doctor of Philosophy in Public Policy and Economics from the University of Missouri-Columbia. He is a seasoned academic researcher and his apt leadership has taught YEF to dream big, take bigger strides and develop a mindset of making an impact right from being a student. The results of his inputs are there for everyone to see in the form of YEF’s success.
